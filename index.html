ABOUT ME

Hello! My name is Jasmine and I am a 2nd year mechanical engineering student at UCLA. I was born in Cairo, Egypt and my culture significantly influences who I am. I hope to eventually be able to use my engineering skills to provide my country with more advanced technology and improve living conditions for those in Egypt. In terms of academics, I am interested in the overlap of circuitry and mechanical design. My interest in mathematics plays an important role in this, as it underpins core principles in electrical circuits and mechanical systems. Outside of my academic pursuits, I am both an avid reader and a meticulous artist, which further fuels my creativity and attention to detail in my engineering projects.
