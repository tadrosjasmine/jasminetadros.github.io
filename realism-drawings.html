MUHAMMAD ALI






This drawing of Muhammad Ali facing Sonny Liston captures the iconic moment where Ali shows his confidence and determination. This piece not only pays tribute to a legendary figure but also embodies his qualities of strength and perseverance that I admire and strive to emulate in my own life.


KOBE BRYANT






This drawing of Kobe Bryant was created as a tribute to his legacy after his passing. I have always admired Kobe's work ethic and mentality. I aspire to apply the same level of commitment and excellence he demonstrated in basketball to everything I do, both professionally and personally.
